# 每日挑战#19 -把数字变成文字

> 原文:[https://dev . to/the practical dev/daily-challenge-19-turn-numbers-into-words-1 bie](https://dev.to/thepracticaldev/daily-challenge-19-turn-numbers-into-words-1bie)

我们还有一个来自 MMMAAANNN 的关于 Codewars 的挑战。今天，您需要:

> 转一个给定的数(一个> 0 的整数，< 1000) into the equivalent English words.
> 例如:
> 
> word ify(1)= = " one "
> word ify(12)= = " twelve "
> word ify(17)= = " seventeen "
> word ify(56)= = " fifty sixth "
> word ify(90)= = "九十"
> wordify(326) == "三百二十六"
