Explicit flex-grow value: certain items fill the surrounding space. align-self: lets your flex items override align-items. align-self: flex-end; puts the item at the end of the flex container.](img/bbfddfc18d45509f8c3ba8d64fc6550f.png)](https://res.cloudinary.com/practicaldev/image/fetch/s--tPv04Lyp--/c_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cfl_progressive%2Cq_auto%2Cw_880/https://thepracticaldev.s3.amazonaws.com/i/8b3cw7h1wa7ucxfv1qi5.png)
